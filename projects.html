Research Area

Throughout my study, my focus was on the design and development of sustainable technology, specifically plastic shredders and heat exchangers for summer cooling in the context of Nepal. The project showcased successful results by effectively shredding waste plastic into small pellets for recycling. Additionally, the heat exchanger was engineered to harness geothermal energy for summer cooling, with the aim of potentially replacing household air conditioners in Nepal. Despite its considerable size, the project yielded promising results.
